Tutorial

  • The game has an economy based on Claude Coins. You earn coins by defeating enemies during the waves. Coins are required to buy new towers and upgrade them.

  • To buy a new tower, click the Buy button and choose an available spot on the map. An indicator will guide you to valid placement locations.

  • When you click on a tower, you have two options: sell it or upgrade it. Each tower can be upgraded up to two levels, requiring more coins.
